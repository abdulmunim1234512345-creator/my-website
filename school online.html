Online Recording

During recent online classes at Sidded Public School, it has been observed that some students engaged in inappropriate verbal conduct. Their language at times has not met the expected standards of respect and discipline required in a classroom environment, even when conducted virtually. This behavior disrupts the learning atmosphere and reflects poorly on the seriousness with which the sessions are approached. It is recommended that students be reminded of digital classroom etiquette, the importance of respectful communication, and the consequences of using offensive or disrespectful language.
